About the company

Bayline Solutions, LLC was formed in January 2019 by Kurt Bodiker on the principle of providing customers the right resources to fit their mission and budget. We can help ensure you have the right resources your mission will need over it's lifetime, allowing you to properly execute your budget.

About the owner

Kurt Bodiker founded Bayline Solutions, LLC, located in Severna Park, Maryland, after 23 years of experience doing DevOps and Software Engineering. Kurt's experience began when he joined the U.S. Air Force as a Communications-Computer Systems Programmer. After 8 years of active duty service, Kurt first transferred into the Air Force Reserve and then into the Maryland Air National Guard where he finished his career and retired with 20 years of honorable service as a Cyber Operations Superintendent. Today, Kurt continues his career as a DevOps Engineer supporting federal missions.

A few words from Kurt

Over the course of my career, I've been an integral part in the rapid adoption and evolution of development and deployment strategies employed on teams of various sizes. Before it was called DevOps, those roles and responsibilities fell onto me, usually because I was either the single guy or because I lived closer to work than anyone else. These experiences have helped me learn to critically analyze and assess the mission and it's needs. I don't believe in "one size fits all" solutions. Every mission is unique and so are it's requirements. Let Bayline Solutions help you navigate your mission to success.
